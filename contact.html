Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can contact us via WhatsApp, email, or phone to discuss your order. Alternatively, fill out our quote request form and we will get back to you within 24 hours.

How long does it take to deliver?

Turnaround time depends on the service and order size. Printing orders typically take 1–3 business days. Websites take 1–4 weeks depending on the package. We'll confirm timelines when you place your order.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, for larger projects like web design packages we can arrange payment plans. Please contact us to discuss your budget and we'll find a solution that works for you.

Can I provide my own design files?

Absolutely! You can supply your own artwork in PDF, AI, EPS, PNG (300+ DPI) formats. If you need us to design from scratch, we offer that too — just include it in your quote request.

Do you serve clients outside your immediate area?

Yes! Digital services (web design, graphic design, digital marketing) are available nationwide. For physical products, we can discuss delivery options with you directly.
